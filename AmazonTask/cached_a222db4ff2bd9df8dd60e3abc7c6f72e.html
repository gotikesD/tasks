4.0 out of 5 starsiPhone 6 review
By Jacob Masching on September 25, 2015
Color: Gold
I am a college student and I purchased an iPhone 6 a few months ago. This is my first smartphone that I have ever owned before, so it was quite a leap to go from a flip phone to a smartphone. I was concerned that I was not going to be able to figure out how to use all the phone’s features properly, but the iPhone 6 has a simplistic layout, which makes it an easy phone to use. Within the first day of owning the phone, I had the phone almost entirely figured out.

Concerns:
One of my concerns with the iPhone 6 is dropping the phone and breaking or cracking the screen. I have broken a few phones from accidentally dropping them in the past and the iPhone 6 looks less durable than my other phones. To prevent the chance of breaking it, you should buy an iPhone case to protect it. I have dropped my phone a few times in the past months of owning the iPhone, and the case has prevented the phone from breaking and the screen from cracking.

Positives:
The phone has excellent battery life and can last up to one to two days without needing to be charged depending on how much you use your phone. I use my phone often throughout the day and not having to charge it all the time is wonderful and time saving. The size of the phone is appropriate and will fit in your hand well. The camera on the phone can take high quality pictures and videos. It has a larger screen than the past iPhones. The slightly larger screen is great for playing games or watching videos to pass the time. When playing games, it does not take forever to load and during the games it does not lag or freeze.

The iPhone 6 is a great product. I use it every day and have never had any problems with it. It is a high quality phone and has made my life a lot easier since I bought it. I would recommend the iPhone 6 to anyone who may be interested in purchasing a smart phone.

5.0 out of 5 starsIf you are like me, you are torn between the iPhone 6 ...
By Amazon Customer on December 2, 2015
Color: Gold Verified Purchase
If you are like me, you are torn between the iPhone 6 and the iPhone 6 Plus. Lucky for you, I own the iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus, and the iPhone 5s. I'll compare the three models and hopefully will help you make the hard decision of which version to buy.

I have owned every iPhone since the 3G. I was very excited to learn the iPhone was getting a bigger screen, but when I learned there was two choices, immediately I was torn between getting the 6 or the Plus. I went as far as making a paper cutout with exact dimensions and going to a store to mess with the Galaxy Note 3 as they are similar in size (this was before the iPhone release). I ended up going with the iPhone 6 Plus and pre-ordered it, followed by the iPhone 6 a few weeks after launch for my wife.

The first thing I must admit is everything about this phone is amazing. There are no 90* angles, no rough edges, no flaw on the phone. Everything is smooth and looks gorgeous. The screen is crystal clear, it is extremely light, and it just feels great. The size of the screen is massive - it honestly is bigger than you can imagine without actually holding it in your hand. The increase in size over the older models and even the 6 is excellent for watching movies, reading books, surfing the web, using Facebook, and much more. Having the extra screen size allows you to use your email and text app in landscape mode, which is helpful. Everything was great for me, at first. I found the more I used it, especially for longer durations in the day, it becomes quite uncomfortable to hold in your hand all day, especially if you text or email a lot. Unless you have a massive hand, one handed iPhone use is most certainly a think of the past.
