3.0 out of 5 starsNot My Best Amazon Basics Purchase
By Daniel Burke on November 27, 2015
Size: 6 FeetColor: White Verified Purchase
I'm a big fan of Amazon Basics products, and I have two shorter AB Lightning cables for my iPhone. This time I nearly bought another like I had before, but I fell for the "There is a newer version of this item," and bought the new and improved version. All I can say is if this is improved, Amazon - stop improving things.

My two other cables are thin and flexible, with reasonably sized connectors. The Lightning end of this cable is twice the size of an Apple cable, and quite a bit larger than the previous AB versions too.

The diameter of the cord itself it 25% larger than the Apple OEM cable, but here's the problem: It's as stiff as a coil spring. Whereas my other Amazon cables are easy to coil and store, this thing is like a piece of Romex electrical wire.

All in all, it works fine, and for 8 bucks I can't find too much to complain about. But it's definitely not the same quality of product I've purchased before.
