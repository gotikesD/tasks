5.0 out of 5 starsQuality Power Bank
By Brit on October 4, 2015
Color: Black
I had always wanted to purchase a portable battery charger to ensure I do not run out of battery life while on-the-go. I was definitely happy with this product. It is lightweight (about 3.5 ounces) and compact in size, so it is easy to bring with you in your pocket or purse/bag. The body of the battery charger seems durable, as it is made of aluminum. The product has a lithium ion battery (rechargeable) and has 4 blue LED lights to display the charge of said battery (4 lights on is 100% ~ 75%, 3 lights on is 75% ~ 50%, 2 lights on is 50% ~ 25%, and 1 light on is 25% ~ 0%). The battery also turns itself off when it is not in use and/or when your phone is fully charged, thereby saving battery life for the portable charger. I charged my iPhone 6 when it was at 50% battery life and it took approximately 40 minutes to charge to 100%; therefore, when my phone is dead, it should take about an hour and 20 minutes to fully charge. Although, the box says the charging time is anywhere from 3-4 hours, so I am sure that time of charge is variable. The cable that was included with the purchase is a micro USB cable. Keep in mind this is NOT compatible with iPhones. However, all you have to do is replace the micro USB cable with the cable you use to charge your iPhone to ensure the charging of the phone. If your phone can be charged with a micro USB cable, then you will not have this issue. The product has a space for the micro USB cable for charging purposes and another space for your charging cable (if not a micro USB cable). Other reviewers had mentioned their portable battery charger came fully charged; this was not the case with mine since only 2 LED lights were lit upon arrival.
