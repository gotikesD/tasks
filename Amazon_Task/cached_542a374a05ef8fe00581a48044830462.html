5.0 out of 5 starsThis is an awesome battery power bank
By TKV TOP 1000 REVIEWER on November 23, 2015
Color: grey
This is an awesome battery power bank. It chargers pretty quick from dead to full in about 2 1/2 hours and does not have to be recharged for many weeks if it is not being used. Not sure the stand by time but I did not use mine for a 3 week period and it was still fully charged. The battery is 3350 mAh so I was able to charge my Samsung Galaxy S6 2 1/2 times before I had to recharge the power bank. I will say the charging speed for my S6 would be a medium speed. Not as fast as if plugged into the wall but faster than my other power bank. It charges with a standard Micro to USB cable and they have included a short one. I like that it is small enough that I can carry it in my pocket. If you charge your phone while it your pocket it will heat up that area a bit and can get to be a bit much on a hot summer day but really nice on a cold winters day. I purchased this power bank at a discount for me to test and leave my honest and unbiased review.

This rated at 1 amps for it is bet for charging android type devices. The new iPhone and iPad require 2.0 amps or higher to charge so this is not best for those products.

I also tested with the following and it was able to charge without any issues:
Samsung Galaxy S6
Samsung Galaxy S4
Samsung Galaxy S4 Mini
Samsung Galaxy S3
Several different brands of Bluetooth speakers of ear phones.
